P[2] [Normal]: This project builds an operations intelligence platform for Skyhaven International Airport (SHI), handling 40 million passengers annually across 180 daily flights and 3 terminals. The platform ingests heterogeneous data from the Airport Operations Database (AODB), gate management systems, baggage tracking, and IoT passenger counter sensors — transforming them into actionable operations dashboards and regulatory compliance reports.
P[4] [Normal]: The pipeline delivers real-time delay propagation alerts, gate utilization optimization, baggage misconnect risk flags, and the DGCA-mandated On-Time Performance (OTP) report — all currently produced manually or not at all.
P[7] [Normal]: Skyhaven Airport faces the following operational data challenges:
P[9] [List Paragraph]: No Delay Early Warning: Cascading delays discovered at departure time, not 2 hours before when interventions are still possible
P[10] [List Paragraph]: Manual OTP Reporting: DGCA On-Time Performance reports compiled manually — 3-day lag, error-prone
P[11] [List Paragraph]: Gate Allocation Inefficiency: Gates assigned based on tribal knowledge — 40% average idle time on some gates
P[12] [List Paragraph]: Baggage Misconnections: Transfer bag risk discovered only after flight departs — average 320 misconnects/month
P[13] [List Paragraph]: Security Queue Blindness: Terminal operations cannot predict when 40-minute security queues will form
P[14] [List Paragraph]: Data Silos: Flight, baggage, passenger, and IoT data in four completely separate systems
P[16] [Normal]: Business Objectives:
P[17] [List Paragraph]: Detect delay propagation risk 2+ hours before scheduled departure
P[18] [List Paragraph]: Automate OTP report generation — available by 06:00 AM for daily operations briefing
P[19] [List Paragraph]: Reduce baggage misconnections by 30% through proactive risk alerts
P[20] [List Paragraph]: Enable gate utilization optimization — target < 25% average idle time
P[21] [List Paragraph]: Predict security queue lengths 60 minutes ahead using IoT + flight schedule data
